Installation:
Simply copy the DigLaB folder to the desired location.
Do not change the location or names of the Templates and Parameters directories.

Resolved issues related to acrobat reader security settings:
    - Go to Edit/Preferences
    - Open the Protection section (reinforced)
    - Check that the box "Enable protected mode at startup" is not checked.
    - Add the DigLaB folder with its full path to the preferred locations.
      To do this click at the bottom of the page on "Add a folder path".
      Search the DigLaB folder on your computer

Don't forget to validate your modifications.
